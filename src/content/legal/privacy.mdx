---
title: Privacy
effective: 2026-04-21
---

LumaSync does not track you. There is no account, no telemetry, and no
cloud sync. Your Hue bridge credentials stay in
`~/.config/lumasync/app.json` on your machine.

This website (`lumasync.app`) uses first-party, cookie-free analytics
(Umami, self-hosted). No personal data is collected; no data is shared
with third parties.
